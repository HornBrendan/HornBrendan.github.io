Almond Green Salad

Ingredients

1/2 pound bacon
2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
3 tablespoons of honey
1/2 dry hot mustard
1/2 teaspoon celery salt
1/2 teaspoon ground paprika
1/4 cup olive oil
1 head red leaf lettuce torn into bite size pieces
1 can of mandarin oranges, drained
1 bunch green onions, diced
3/4 cup silvered almonds

directions

1. in medium skillet over medium heat, cook bacon until evenly browned, Drain cool and crumble 2.to make the dressing thorougly blend the vinegar, honey, dry mustard, celery salt, peprika and olive oil 3. Place lettuce, oranges, green onion, bacon, and almonds in serving bowl. Toss with dressing and sserve
